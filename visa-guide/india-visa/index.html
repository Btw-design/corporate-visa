Overview

India Visa Information

India offers a range of visa options for business travelers, tourists, and corporate visitors. The India e-Visa program has simplified the application process for citizens of over 160 countries, allowing for online applications without visiting an embassy.

For corporate travelers, the Business Visa (B-1) is the most suitable option, permitting multiple entries and longer stays for business meetings, conferences, trade fairs, and exploratory visits.

FAQ

India Visa FAQs

India offers e-Tourist Visa, e-Business Visa, e-Medical Visa, Regular Tourist Visa, Business Visa (B-1), Employment Visa, Student Visa, and Conference Visa. The e-Visa is the most convenient for short-term visits.

e-Visa processing takes 3-5 business days. Regular visas processed through the embassy take 7-10 business days. Business visas may take 10-15 business days depending on the applicant's nationality.

Required documents include a valid passport with 6+ months validity, completed visa application form, recent passport-size photographs, business invitation letter from the Indian company, cover letter from employer, proof of business registration, and financial statements.

Yes, the India e-Business Visa is available for business purposes including meetings, conferences, trade fairs, and business deals. It allows multiple entries and is valid for up to 1 year.

e-Visas cannot be extended. Regular Business Visas (B-1) can be extended at the Foreigners Regional Registration Office (FRRO) in India. Extensions are typically granted for up to 6 months at a time.
