Safety First

Visa & Travel Safety Tips

Protect Your Documents

Never share passport copies or visa documents with unverified agents. Always verify credentials through BTW Visa.

Apply Early

Submit visa applications at least 4–6 weeks before travel. Rush processing is not always available or reliable.

Verify Official Channels

Always verify visa requirements through official embassy websites or trusted platforms like BTW Visa.
